What to expect

On the next few pages, you will be making a series of choices between bundles of tasks. One of your bundles will be randomly assigned for you to work on at the end of the experiment. Majority of your bonus payment depends on how well you perform at this bundle of tasks, therefore, choose your bundles carefully.

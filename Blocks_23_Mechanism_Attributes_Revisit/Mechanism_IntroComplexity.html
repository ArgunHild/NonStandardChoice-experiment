How this part works

👀

What to expect

On the next few pages, you will be making a series of choices between bundles of Tasks. Think of each page as if you are choosing from a menu.

When the choice phase is over, the computer will randomly select one of those menus and the bundle you chose in that menu. You will then complete every Task in that bundle. You earn a bonus of {{C.Bonus_max}} EUR only if you reach the required score on every Task in that bundle (see Task difficulty below).

The bonus you can earn here is large, therefore, try to choose the best fitting bundles for you.
⚙️

How to make a selection

{% if mechanism == 'Binary' %}

There will be a menu containing 3 – 7 bundles.

Once you click Start the mechanism, you will be shown two bundles at a time. In each round simply choose the bundle you prefer. You will make a handful of these quick comparisons until the system can determine your clear favourite based on your answers.

{% else %}

There will be a menu containing 3 – 7 bundles.

Once you click Start the mechanism, the bundles will appear and you can then choose the bundle you prefer. {% endif %}

🧩

Bundle complexity

You will work through three blocks one after the other: Low → Medium → High. Each step adds one more Task to every bundle, so choices get a little richer as you progress.

Complexity level Tasks per bundle Example
Low1 Task😃2
Medium2 Tasks🔢1 + 😃2
High3 Tasks🔢1 + 😃2 + 🔍2
🎯

Task difficulty

Each Task appears at one of three difficulty levels (shown as a subscript as in the previous block):

SubscriptLabelScore needed
1Easy≥ 30 % correct
2Medium≥ 50 % correct
3Hard≥ 80 % correct

Example: Quiz 2 has 30 questions → you must answer at least 15 correctly.

Bonus rule: You receive the bonus only if you meet the threshold on every Task in the realised bundle. Difficulty is independent of bundle complexity—so even a Low bundle can contain a hard Task.

It is important that you understand these instructions. On the next page, you will see some comprehension questions about these instructions.

These instructions will be available to you via the button at the bottom of the screen.

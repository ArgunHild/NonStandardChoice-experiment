How this part works

👀

What to expect

On the next few pages, you will be making a series of choices between bundles of tasks. When the choice phase is over, the computer will randomly select one of those menus and the bundle you chose in that menu. You will then complete every task in that bundle. You earn a bonus of XX EUR only if you hit the required score on every task in that bundle (see Task difficulty below).
⚙️

How to make a selection

{% if mechanism == 'binary' %}

You will first see a menu containing 3 – 7 bundles; at that point nothing is clickable. Click Start the mechanism when you are ready to begin.

After that, you will be shown two bundles at a time. In each round simply choose the bundle you prefer. You will make a handful of these quick comparisons until the system can determine your clear favourite.

{% else %}

You will first see a menu containing 3 – 7 bundles (nothing is clickable yet). When you are ready, click Start the mechanism. The same bundles will re‑appear and you can then choose the bundle you prefer.

{% endif %}
🧩

Bundle complexity

You will work through three blocks one after the other: Low → Medium → High. Each step adds one more task to every bundle, so choices get a little richer as you progress.

Complexity level Tasks per bundle Example
Low1 task😃2
Medium2 tasks🔢1 + 😃2
High3 tasks🔢1 + 😃2 + 🔍2
🎯

Task difficulty

Each task appears at one of three difficulty levels (shown as a subscript as in the previous block):

SubscriptLabelScore needed
1Easy≥ 25 % correct
2Medium≥ 50 % correct
3Hard≥ 75 % correct

Example: Quiz 2 has 30 questions → you must answer at least 15 correctly.

Bonus rule: You receive the bonus only if you meet the threshold on every task in the realised bundle. Difficulty is independent of bundle complexity—so even a Low bundle can contain a hard task.

These instructions will be available to you via the button at the bottom of the screen.

It is important that you understand these instructions. On the next page, you will see some comprehension questions about these instructions.

The Math‑Memory Task

You will see 16 grey boxes arranged in a grid. Hidden inside every box are:

  • a simple addition (for example 1 + 1 or 2 + 3)
  • a heart that is either ❤️ red or 🖤 black

Your goal is to find matching pairs. Two boxes are a pair when both the sum and the heart color are the same.

🧩

Rounds

  • Each round starts with 16 closed boxes → 8 possible pairs.
  • Click one box to reveal it, then click a second box.
  • If the two boxes make a pair, they stay open. If not, they close again after a short pause.
  • When you have found all 8 pairs, the next round appears automatically.
  • You can play up to 3 rounds in total.

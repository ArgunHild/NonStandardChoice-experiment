The MathMemory Game

On your screen, you will see 16 gray boxes. Each box hides:

  • A simple addition problem (e.g., 1+1, 2+3)
  • A colored heart (either ❤️ red or 🖤 black)

Your task is to find matching pairs of boxes by clicking to reveal them. A pair is a match if:

  1. The sums are the same, and
  2. The heart color is the same
🖼️

🧩

Sets

  • Each set consists of 16 boxes → 8 possible matching pairs.
  • Once you find all matches, a new set will appear automatically.
  • You can complete up to 3 sets total.
🎯

Scoring

  • Each correctly matched pair earns you 1 point.
  • Maximum score: 24 points (8 per set × 3 sets)

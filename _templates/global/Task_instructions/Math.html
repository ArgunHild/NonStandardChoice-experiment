The MathMemory Game

Basics

On your screen you will see 16 gray boxes. Each box hides a simple addition (like '1+1' or '2+3') and a colored heart (red ‚ù§Ô∏è or black üñ§). By clicking on a box you can reveal its content. Your task is to click on the boxes to find matching pairs. Two boxes form a matching pair if both of the following are true:
  1. The sums are the same.
  2. The hearts are the same color.
In other words, two boxes are a matching pair if both the sums and the colors are the same.

Examples

Sets

You will start with a set of 16 boxes, which means there are 8 matching pairs in total. Each time you find a matching pair, the two boxes will disappear. After finding all matching pairs in a set, a new set of 16 boxes will appear. There is a maximum of 3 sets.

Scoring

You earn one point for each matching pair you find. There are a maximum of 24 points you can score.

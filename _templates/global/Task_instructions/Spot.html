The Spot-the-Difference Game

In this task, you will see two similar pictures side by side. Your job is to find 10 differences between them and click on each difference on the right picture.

🖼️

Example

In this example, three differences are marked: the missing bridge, the absent third child, and the different colored shirt on the first child.

🎯

Scoring

You can place up to 10 marks on the right picture. You may change or remove your marks at any time. Each correctly identified difference counts as one problem solved.

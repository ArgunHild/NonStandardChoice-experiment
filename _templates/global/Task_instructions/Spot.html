The Spot‑the‑Difference Task

Two almost‑identical pictures will appear side by side. There are 10 hidden differences. Your task is to click on every difference in the right‑hand picture.

🖼️

Example

In this example, three differences are marked: the missing bridge, the absent third child, and the different shirt colour on the first child.

🔍

How to Play

  • You may place up to 10 markers on the right picture — one for each difference.
  • Click once to add a marker. Click the same spot again to remove it.
  • You can move or delete markers at any time until you submit.
  • Only the right picture accepts clicks; clicking the left picture has no effect.

The SpotTheDifference Game

Basics

In this task, you will see two pictures. The picture on the left and the picture on the right are very similar but there are 10 differences. Your task is to find these differences and click on them on the right picture.

An example is depicted below. In this example, three differences are marked: on the right picture the bridge and the third kid are missing, and the first kid has a different shirt

Example


Scoring

You can place at most 10 marks and you can change or remove them at any time. Each correctly marked difference counts as one problem solved.

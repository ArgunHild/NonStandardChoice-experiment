The Quiz Game

Basics

There will be a maximum of 30 multiple-choice questions. These questions are from various domains such as Art, Languages, Geography, Technology, History, etc. You have to choose the one correct answer out of 4 options.

These answers become clickable only after 4 seconds of having seen the question. For each question, you have a maximum of 10 seconds to answer. If you do not answer within these 10 seconds, the next question will be displayed.

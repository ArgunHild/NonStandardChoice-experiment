The Emotion Recognition Task

You will see pictures of people’s faces showing different feelings. For each picture, choose the emotion that best matches the expression.

🖼️

Example Picture

Correct answer: “Amusement”.

🎯

How It Works

  • You will see 10 pictures in total.
  • Each picture comes with 4 emotion labels to choose from.
  • Select one label by clicking or tapping it. Clicking before the labels appear has no effect.

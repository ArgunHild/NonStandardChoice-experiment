How This Part Works

👀

What to Expect

In the next few pages you will see several menus of task bundles. Each menu shows 3 – 7 bundles, and you will pick one bundle from every menu.

After you finish the study, the computer will randomly choose one of your menus and then randomly pick one of your chosen bundles. You will work on that bundle, and most of your bonus payment will depend on how well you do. Therefore, please choose each bundle carefully.

⚙️

Making a Selection

{% if mechanism == 'binary' %}

First you will see the whole menu; nothing is clickable yet. Click Start the mechanism when you are ready.

You will then be shown two bundles at a time. In each round click the bundle you prefer. After a few quick rounds the system will identify your favorite bundle automatically.

{% else %}

First you will see the whole menu; nothing is clickable yet. When you are ready, click Start the mechanism.

The same menu will re‑appear and you can now click the single bundle you prefer.

{% endif %}
🧩

Bundle Complexity

You will face three blocks of menus one after another: Low → Medium → High. With each new block, one extra task is added to every bundle, so your choices become richer.

Complexity Leveltasks per Bundle
Low1 task
Medium2 tasks
High3 tasks
🎯

Task Difficulty

Every task can appear in three difficulty levels. The level number is shown as a sub‑script next to the task icon.

Level NumberDifficulty LabelScore Needed
1Easy≥ 30 % correct
2Medium≥ 50 % correct
3Hard≥ 80 % correct

Example: Quiz₂ (Quiz at medium difficulty) contains 30 questions, and you must answer at least 15 correctly.

You earn the bundle’s bonus only if you meet the required score for every task inside that bundle.

Difficulty is independent of bundle complexity: even a Low bundle can hold a hard task.

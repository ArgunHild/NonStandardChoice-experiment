Below is a short reminder of what each attribute means:

🧠 Cognitive Ease

How effortless the task felt—how little mental effort, memory load, or switching between steps it required.

🎮 Engagement

How enjoyable or stimulating the task felt—whether it was fun or kept your interest.

💡 Confidence

How sure you felt that you could do the task well.

⏱️ Time Efficiency

How quick and smooth the task felt and how long it took.

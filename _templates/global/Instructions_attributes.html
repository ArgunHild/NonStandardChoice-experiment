Task Attribute Overview

In the next few steps, you'll be asked to reflect on different aspects of the tasks you completed earlier — things like how mentally demanding, enjoyable, or time-consuming they felt. These aspects are called attributes, and you'll use them to rank the tasks based on your own experience.

Below is a short overview of the attributes you’ll be using to guide your rankings:

🧠 Cognitive Load

How mentally demanding the task feels — including focus, memory, and switching between steps. Also considers fatigue from repetition.

🎮 Engagement

How enjoyable or stimulating the task feels. Some tasks may feel more fun or interesting than others.

💡 Confidence

How confident you feel in your ability to do the task well, based on your skills and perceived difficulty.

⏱️ Time Efficiency

How efficient the task feels — how long it takes, how many clicks are needed, and how quickly it moves.
